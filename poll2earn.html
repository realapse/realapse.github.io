What is B3TR?

B3TR is the native token of B3trSurv, designed to reward participants for contributing valuable insights through our surveys and polls. Each B3TR token you earn represents your engagement and influence in shaping the data landscape.

What is VeBetterDAO?

VeBetterDAO is our governing entity built on the VeChain blockchain, ensuring transparency and fairness in decision-making processes. It allows B3trSurv users to have a say in the platform’s development and the distribution of rewards.

VeChain’s Role

VeChain provides the robust blockchain infrastructure that powers B3trSurv. Its secure, scalable, and transparent technology ensures that all data collected is trustworthy and immutable. VeChain’s integration also allows for seamless transactions and smart contract functionality.

Purpose and Utility of B3TR

B3TR tokens can be used within the B3trSurv ecosystem for various purposes:

  • Participation Rewards: Earn B3TR for completing surveys and polls.
  • Access to Insights: Use B3TR to access detailed data insights and reports.
  • Governance: Stake B3TR to participate in VeBetterDAO’s decision-making processes.
  • Premium Features: Unlock premium features and advanced analytics tools using B3TR.

Possibilities and Impact

B3trSurv aims to be a force for good by:

  • Combating Misinformation: Providing a transparent and accessible ledger of public information to counteract misinformation and disinformation.
  • Empowering Users: Giving everyday people the opportunity to contribute to and benefit from valuable data insights.
  • Promoting Transparency: Ensuring all data is public and verifiable, fostering trust in the information presented.
  • Stimulating the Economy: Distributing rewards in the form of B3TR tokens to stimulate economic activity within the ecosystem.
